7 Cheap Indonesian Web Hosts in 2019

There are lots of cheap web hosting out there, which even offer prices of Rp. 5,000 / month. But are you sure that cheap web hosting can support your needs? I know that many websites and blogs can work well on cheap hosting packages, but when you are looking for a cheap web host, of course you also don't want a web host with "cheap services" too, right?

Here are 7 Cheap Indonesian Web Hosts in 2019

1. Jetorbit - Cheap Hosting with the Best Performance

This web hosting company is based in Sleman, Yogyakarta and has been operating since 2012. For those of you who are familiar with the world of websites, you might only hear the name because it is still relatively new.

But make no mistake, although it is rather new, Jetorbit can be an option for those of you who intend to build a blog / website.

Jetorbit is a web hosting service provider with pretty good performance but at a very affordable price.

Not much info can be explored because even though it has been operating for almost 8 years, they don't even include an 'About Us' page on their official website.

ADVANTAGES:
  • Fast average page load time (0.907 seconds)
  • Average Stable Uptime (99.94%)
  • Free Divi (page builder for WordPress)
  • Free Domain Hosting and Free Migration
DEFICIENCY:
  • Super Tight Warranty Terms
  • Support Not Too Good
  • Backup Feature Is Not Optimal
  • Late Payment Fee After 5 Days Charging + 30% Penalty
Visit Jerobit

2. Jagoan Hosting - Hosting at the Cheapest Cost

Jagoan Hosting is a hosting that is quite popular in Indonesia. Maybe most of you already know him.

Jagoan Hosting was founded in 2007 and claims to have served more than 70,000 websites. An extraordinary achievement is not it?

Jagoan Hosting was created to serve entry-level customers who still want good quality hosting, but at a low price.

ADVANTAGES:
  • Fairly Fast Page Load Time (Speed) (1,035 seconds)
  • Fairly Good Support
  • Has a Complete Knowledge Base and Hosting Dictionary
  • Hosting Purchase Guarantee for 30 Days
  • Free Migration Hosting
  • Free Daily, Weekly and Monthly Backups
DEFICIENCY:
  • Unstable Servers (Bad Uptime Average - 99.60%)
  • "FAME" Package Has Resource Limitations
  • Penalty For Data Restore Fee If Late Paying Hosting Extension Bill
Visit Jagoan Hosting

3. Niagahoster - Web Host with Good Support

Niagahoster is hosting that is currently on the rise. They are still relatively new in the web hosting industry competition map in Indonesia.

But its development can be said to be very fast, because in these 5 years it has gathered more than 52,000 customers from all over Indonesia.

Good achievement. But what are the strengths of Niagahoster? Is the uptime stable? The server is fast? Or is the support good?

ADVANTAGES:
  • The page load time is quite fast (1,021 seconds)
  • Good service support because this site has a conversation system where you no longer need to send support tickets for technical and non-technical questions (8/10).
  • Flexible hosting package options
  • Have a complete guide or basic knowledge (+ video tutorial)
  • 30 day guarantee without conditions
  • Free domain forever and free hosting migration
DEFICIENCY:
  • Average unstable uptime (99.72%)
  • The 'Baby' package has limitations
  • The backup feature has limitations (backup is only done once a week)
  • Must subscribe with a long duration (more than 3 years) to get the cheapest hosting price (IDR 10,000 / month)
  • Cannot choose server location when ordering hosting. The server will be random (Jakarta or Singapore)
Visit Niagahoster

4. IdCloudHost

IdCloudHost is also a relatively new hosting. They were founded in June 2015.

They claim to have more than 40,000 service users from all over Indonesia and abroad.

They claim to have more than 40,000 service users from all over Indonesia and abroad.

As the name, IdCloudHost already uses cloud technology, no longer using classic technology. From what I read on its official page, IdCloudHost says that they use the fastest processor on the Cloud Hosting market, the Intel E5 Processor and also use SSD in their storage.

They even claim a 99.99% uptime guarantee.

ADVANTAGES:
  • Fairly Fast Page Load Time (1,187 seconds)
  • Fairly Good Support Services.
  • Flexible Hosting Package Options (+ Also Available Hosting Package Options Using Plesk Panels)
  • Giving 30 Days Guarantee Without Conditions
  • 30 day guarantee without conditions
  • Free Domain and Migration or Transfer Hosting
  • Running Social Programs To Help Many People
DEFICIENCY:
  • Poor Uptime Average (99.73%)
  • There is no Add On Domain Feature in the Personal Package
  • The backup feature has a weakness, where the backup is not done every day, but periodically (4x in a week)
Visit IdCloudHost

5. ArdHosting

ArdHosting is probably the oldest hosting in Indonesia. They have been established since 2000.

It must be recognized that they are the most experienced services in the Indonesian web hosting industry.

But, has its experience for almost 2 decades made ArdHosting the best hosting in Indonesia?

Unfortunately no.

The average active and loading time of ArdHosting is good, but fails on the Load Impact factor.

Another disadvantage is that ArdHosting only provides a guarantee for 7 days where a standard warranty in the web hosting industry must be given a minimum of 30 days.

ADVANTAGES:
  • Fairly Fast Page Load Time (1,017 seconds)
  • Fairly Good Support Services.
  • Average stable uptime (99.94%)
  • Has the main features that are qualified (pure SSD storage, Layered backup, LiteSpeed, CloudLinux)
  • Free domains on all hosting packages and free migration or transfer hosting
  • It has daily, weekly and monthly backup features
DEFICIENCY:
  • Guarantees are only given for 7 days
  • There are fees for some actions such as downgrading the hosting package to be charged IDR 50,000, replace the server IDR 50,000, etc.
Visit ArdHosting

6. Dapur Hosting

Dapur Hosting has been established since 2005. Most of you probably already know it. They are quite popular in Indonesia. So, no wonder the information that I got from the HostAdvice site is that they are included in the top 10 hosting companies with the most clients in Indonesia.

Dapur Hosting not only has a server with a stable average uptime (99.93%), but also a record of average page load time which is quite good, which is 1.04 seconds. Plus, they also have pretty good support.

ADVANTAGES:
  • Fairly Fast Page Load Time (1.04 seconds)
  • Good Support Services.
  • Average stable uptime (99.93%)
  • Free hosting migration or transfer
  • 30 day hosting guarantee (with certain conditions)
  • There is a hosting trial for 7 days.
DEFICIENCY:
  • Hosting payment cycle of at least 1 year
  • There is a fee for downgrade hosting
  • 'Package A' has limited resources
  • The only provider of website hosting that is not uncommon using SSL (note secure)
Visit Dapur Hosting

7. ArdetaMedia

Actually not much can be known about ArdetaMedia. They don't have an 'about us' page. What is clear is that they are still in the same company as Jogja Host.

however, the advantage of ArdetaMedia is the fairly fast page load time, which is 1.09 seconds.

ADVANTAGES:
  • Quite Fast Page Load Time (1.09 seconds)
  • Good Support Services.
  • Free migration or free hosting and domain transfers for certain packages
  • 30 day hosting guarantee (with certain conditions)
DEFICIENCY:
  • Average worse uptime (99.63%)
  • There are fees for some actions that must be free
  • Backup features have limitations
Visit ArdetaMedia
